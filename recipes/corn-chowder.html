Corn Chowder

Home

Description

This is a great low-fat comfort food. The creaminess of this chowder makes it a great meal starter or as the main course!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 cubes vegetable bouillon
  • 2 cups corn
  • 2 cups nondairy milk
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

  2. Add onion and celery; cook until just slightly golden.

  3. Stir in carrots and garlic; cook until garlic is slightly golden.

  4. Bring water to a boil in a pot over high heat. Add bouillon cubes, reduce heat to medium, and stir until cubes are dissolved.

  5. Add corn and onion mixture; cook until vegetables are tender, adding water, if necessary.

  6. Reduce heat to low, pour in 1 cup nondairy milk, stir well, then stir in remaining 1 cup nondairy milk.

  7. Quickly whisk in flour; stir in parsley, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.

  8. Cook until chowder thickens, stirring constantly, 15 to 20 minutes.
